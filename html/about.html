About Of Code Setu

CodeSetu is a free online platform where you can learn computer and coding skills in both Hindi and English, completely free of cost. Our mission is to help every student—whether a complete beginner or someone from a small town—learn web development, programming, and technology skills without any barriers.
Here’s what you’ll find on CodeSetu:
✅ Step-by-step tutorials (HTML, CSS, JS, Node.js, etc.)
✅ Hindi + English explanations for better understanding
✅ Real-world projects and practical coding guidance
✅ A friendly community and help with your doubts

CodeSetu – A bridge to coding, in both language!
